Common questions

I can't sign in — what should I try?

First, double-check your email address for typos. If you're using "Sign in with Apple" or "Sign in with Google," make sure you're using the same account you originally signed up with. Use the "Forgot password" option if you registered with email and password. If nothing works, email us with your account email and we'll look into it.

How do I import a trip from a confirmation email?

Open the Trips tab, tap "Import," and forward your booking email to the personal @gocontinuum.app forwarding address shown on that screen. Continuum parses common airline, hotel, and rental car confirmations automatically. If a parser misses something, you can edit the trip after import.

How do I add a receipt to my expense report?

In the Expenses tab, tap the camera icon to snap a photo of the receipt, or upload an existing image. Continuum extracts the merchant, amount, currency, and date automatically. You can edit anything that looks wrong before saving.

Where is my loyalty status pulled from?

Continuum doesn't connect to airline accounts directly. You enter your tier and balances manually (or import them from booking emails), and we track and display them across programs. We never ask for your airline passwords.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete account. Your trips, expenses, receipts, loyalty data, and email forwarding records are permanently removed within 30 days. You can also email us and we'll do it manually.

Push notifications aren't arriving

Check that notifications are enabled for Continuum in your phone's Settings app, and that you've granted permission inside Continuum's Settings → Notifications. If you're on iOS and using the web app (not the App Store version), you need to add Continuum to your Home Screen for notifications to work.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Your data is yours. Continuum uses a small number of vendors strictly to run the service (Supabase for the database, Vercel for hosting, OpenAI for receipt & email parsing). Details and the full list are in the privacy policy.

Found a bug or have a feature idea?

Please email us — we genuinely want to hear it. Continuum is a small project and user feedback drives the roadmap.
